Privacy Policy for Timeline B

Last Updated: 11 Jan 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Timeline B ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you understand exactly how your information is used. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data when you use our automated video publishing platform.

2. Information We Collect

To provide our AI-generated alternative history services, we collect the following types of information:

A. Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you register, we may collect your email address, username, and password.
  • User Inputs: We collect the text prompts, settings, and historical scenarios you input into Timeline B to generate content.

B. Information from Third Parties (TikTok)

When you choose to link your TikTok account to Timeline B, we access certain information via the TikTok API, subject to your explicit permission:

  • Profile Information: Your TikTok username, display name, and avatar image.
  • Access Tokens: Secure tokens required to authenticate and publish videos to your account.
  • Publishing Data: Status updates regarding the upload success/failure of videos generated by our service.

C. Usage Data

  • Log Data: We collect standard log files when you use the app (IP address, browser type, timestamps).
  • Device Information: We may collect information about the device you use to access the service (device model, OS version).

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data for the specific purposes listed below:

  1. To Provide the Service: To log you in, generate the requested alternative history videos, and automate the publishing process to your TikTok account.
  2. AI Generation: Your text inputs are processed by our AI algorithms (and potentially third-party AI providers) solely to create the video, audio, and script content you requested.
  3. Service Improvement: To analyze usage trends and fix technical bugs.
  4. Communication: To send you service-related notices (e.g., "Video Published Successfully" or "Publication Failed").

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We only share information in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share data with trusted third-party vendors who help us operate our services (e.g., cloud hosting providers like AWS/Google Cloud, or AI model providers). These providers are bound by confidentiality agreements.
  • TikTok: We transmit the final generated video file and necessary metadata to TikTok to fulfill your request to publish content.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or a valid legal process (e.g., a subpoena).

5. TikTok Data Specifics (App Audit Compliance)

In strict adherence to TikTok’s Developer Policy:

  • Limited Use: We use TikTok user data solely for the purpose of functionality explicit to the user (generating and publishing videos). We do not use TikTok data for advertising purposes or build user profiles for surveillance.
  • No Transfer: We do not transfer TikTok user data to data brokers or unrelated third parties.
  • Refresh/Deletion: We respect TikTok’s token expiration and user revocation events. If you unlink your TikTok account, we delete the access tokens associated with that account.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information and generated content only for as long as necessary to provide the Service or as required by law.

  • User Inputs/Videos: Are stored for [Number] days to allow for user download/review, after which they are [Archived/Deleted].
  • TikTok Tokens: Are stored encrypted and only while your account is active and authorized.

7. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures (including encryption in transit and at rest) to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request Deletion of your account and data.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can disconnect Timeline B from your TikTok account at any time via the TikTok app settings ("Security and Login" > "Manage App Permissions").

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email below.
